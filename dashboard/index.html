Natural increase vs net migration, NSW (persons)

02

The well-resourced score

For each SA2 in Sydney–Parramatta, points of interest were harvested from the NSW POI API, filtered to inside-polygon, and stored in PostGIS. The score is a sigmoid of the standardised POI count: Score = S(z_POI). Click any region to locate it on the map.

34 SA2 regions, ranked

lowhigh
Scores span 0.16–0.89. The bottom of the table is dominated by designated non-residential land — Yennora & Smithfield industrial estates, Rookwood Cemetery — not under-served communities. That is the scoring method's key limitation: a raw POI count conflates "few amenities" with "not a residential area".
